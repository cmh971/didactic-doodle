Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

1. Introduction

Sentinel ("the bot", "we", "us") is a Discord bot and companion web dashboard. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why, and what choices you have. By adding the bot to a server or signing in to the dashboard, you agree to this policy.

We keep data collection to the minimum needed to make features work. We do not sell your data, and we do not run advertising or third-party tracking.

2. Information we collect

Depending on which features you use, we may store:

  • Discord account data — your Discord user ID, username, and avatar. When you sign in to the dashboard we also read the list of servers you belong to (via the identify and guilds OAuth scopes) to show which ones you can manage.
  • Google account data — if you choose "Sign in with Google", we receive your email address, name, and profile picture (via the openid, email, and profile scopes). This is used only to verify your identity for the login step.
  • Server configuration — settings you set per server (enabled modules, ticket setup, welcome messages, auto-roles, etc.).
  • Feature data — economy balances, XP/levels, moderation infraction logs, ticket records, and aggregate daily counts of joins/leaves/messages for analytics.
  • Locally stored data — notes and preferences you create in the dashboard are stored in your own browser (localStorage) and never sent to us.

We do not read, log, or store the content of your everyday messages beyond what a feature explicitly requires (for example, a message that triggers the automod filter or a ticket you open).

3. How we use your information

  • To provide bot features (economy, moderation, tickets, leveling, etc.).
  • To authenticate you and confirm you have permission to manage a server.
  • To display statistics and analytics for servers you manage.
  • To keep the service secure and diagnose problems.

We never use your data for advertising, profiling, or resale.

4. Cookies

The dashboard uses a small number of cookies, only for functionality:

  • Session cookie — keeps you logged in after you authenticate. It is signed and HttpOnly.
  • Preference cookies — remember your cookie-consent choice and, if you use the email step, the email you entered on this device.

We use no third-party or advertising cookies. You can decline non-essential cookies via the banner, and you can clear cookies any time in your browser settings.

5. Third-party services

We rely on a few trusted providers to operate:

  • Discord — for the bot platform and OAuth login. See Discord's Privacy Policy.
  • Google — for optional "Sign in with Google". See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Gemini — if you use the AI chat feature, the text you send to the AI is processed by Google's Gemini API to generate a reply.

These providers handle your data under their own privacy policies.

6. Data retention

We keep data only as long as needed to provide the service. Server configuration and feature data persist while the bot is in your server. You can reset a server's data from the dashboard or by using the bot's reset commands, and removing the bot from a server ends new data collection for it.

7. Your rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data. You can also log out at any time, which clears your session. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

8. Children

The service is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age of digital consent in your country). Discord's own Terms require users to meet a minimum age; we rely on that.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach the bot owner via your community's support channel or the contact listed on the bot's Discord profile.

This document is a general, good-faith template for a hobby project and is not legal advice. Review it with a professional before relying on it for a commercial service.
